4H Strategy Schedule & Explanations
What do these 4H strategies mean?
How it works: Every 4 hours, Polymarket opens a new UP/DOWN market for each crypto asset (BTC, ETH, SOL, XRP). The market asks: "Will this asset's price be higher or lower at the end of this 4-hour window?" Our strategies watch these markets and look for specific price patterns that historically predict the outcome with high accuracy.
Entry Minute (e.g. m120, m180): How many minutes into the 4-hour cycle we wait before entering. m120 = we wait 2 hours to see how the market is trending. m180 = we wait 3 hours. Later entries are more certain but have shorter time to resolution.
Direction (UP/DOWN): Which side we bet on. "DOWN 60-75c" means we buy the DOWN token when it costs 60-75 cents, expecting the asset price to drop by cycle end.
Price Band (e.g. 60-75c): We only enter when the token price is in this range. If the DOWN token is at 70c, the market is already ~70% confident it will go down - our strategy confirms this signal is reliable.
Tiers: PLATINUM = highest confidence (best backtest metrics) | GOLD = strong confidence (validated out-of-sample)
Win Rate / LCB: Win Rate is how often the strategy won historically. LCB (Lower Confidence Bound) is the worst-case win rate with 95% statistical confidence - the more conservative measure we actually use for decisions.
